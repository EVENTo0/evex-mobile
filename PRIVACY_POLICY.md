## English Version

### 1. Introduction

Welcome to EVEX ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and related services (collectively, the "Service"). Please read this policy carefully. By using EVEX, you consent to the practices described herein.

### 2. Information We Collect

#### 2.1 Personal Information
- **Account Data:** Name, email address, date of birth, gender, and profile photo when you create an account.
- **Health & Fitness Data:** Weight, height, body measurements, dietary preferences, workout history, calorie intake, and fitness goals.
- **Communication Data:** Messages sent to our AI coaching system and feedback you provide.

#### 2.2 Automatically Collected Information
- **Device Information:** Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers.
- **Usage Data:** App interaction patterns, session duration, features accessed, and crash logs.
- **Location Data:** Approximate location (city-level) for localized content delivery. We do not track precise GPS location.

#### 2.3 Third-Party Data
- Data from integrated fitness wearables or health platforms (only with your explicit consent).
- Authentication data from social login providers (Google, Apple).

### 3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:
- Provide personalized AI coaching, diet plans, and workout recommendations.
- Improve our machine learning models and service quality.
- Process payments and manage subscriptions.
- Send relevant notifications about your fitness goals and progress.
- Ensure security and prevent fraud.
- Comply with legal obligations.

### 4. Data Storage & Security

- All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256).
- Data is stored on secure cloud infrastructure (Supabase/AWS) in compliant regions.
- We implement industry-standard security measures including regular penetration testing and access controls.
- Health data is stored separately from account identifiers where technically feasible.

### 5. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do NOT sell your personal data. We may share information with:
- **Service Providers:** Cloud hosting, analytics, and payment processors who are contractually bound to protect your data.
- **Advertising Partners:** Anonymized, aggregated data only. No personal health information is shared with advertisers.
- **Legal Requirements:** When required by law, court order, or to protect our legal rights.

### 6. Advertising

EVEX displays advertisements through Google AdMob. Advertisers may use device advertising identifiers and anonymized usage patterns. You can opt out of personalized ads through your device settings:
- **Android:** Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of personalized ads
- **iOS:** Settings > Privacy > Apple Advertising > Turn off Personalized Ads

### 7. Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access:** Request a copy of your personal data.
- **Correction:** Update inaccurate information.
- **Deletion:** Request deletion of your account and associated data.
- **Portability:** Export your health and fitness data in a standard format.
- **Withdrawal:** Withdraw consent for optional data processing at any time.

To exercise these rights, contact us at: privacy@evento-tech.com

### 8. Data Retention

- Active account data is retained while your account exists.
- After account deletion, data is purged within 30 days (except where legal retention is required).
- Anonymized analytical data may be retained indefinitely.

### 9. Children's Privacy

EVEX is not intended for users under 16 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If we discover such data, it will be deleted immediately.

### 10. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in servers located outside your country of residence. We ensure adequate protection through standard contractual clauses and compliance with applicable data protection regulations.

### 11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Material changes will be communicated via in-app notification at least 14 days before taking effect.

### 12. Contact Us

Email: privacy@evento-tech.com
Support: support@evento-tech.com
